Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All file conversions happen locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

What formats do you support?

Currently, we support image format conversions including PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP, and BMP.

Is there a file size limit?

File size limits depend on your browser's capabilities. Most modern browsers can handle files up to several hundred megabytes.
